Newspaper Dashboard

Welcome to Article Bits. A Newspaper aggregation, summarization tool that tries to gather and give users a well rounded experience regarding an event or news item. Click on any news story and get a summarized version of the article, definitions from key words/phrases that you might not know, as well as a list of other articles from different source on the same subject. We believe that this will allow you to get a better well rounded experience and understanding of a subject.
